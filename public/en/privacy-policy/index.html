Processing is based on the following legal grounds under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: To know what data we hold about you.

  • Restriction: To request restriction of processing.
  • Portability: To receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: To object to processing based on legitimate interest.

To exercise your rights, send an email to info@falcotech.gr. We will respond within 30 days.
