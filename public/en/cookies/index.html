Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve the browsing experience, and provide information to website owners.

2. Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the basic operation of the website. They cannot be disabled.

  • Cloudflare (__cf_bm): Protection against bots and malicious traffic. Duration: 30 minutes.

2.2 Analytics / Performance Cookies

These help us understand how you use our website so we can improve it.

  • Cloudflare Web Analytics: Anonymous traffic analysis without client-side cookies — data is collected server-side. No personal data is stored.

2.3 Third-Party Cookies

Our website may load resources from third-party providers that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts: For loading web fonts. Google may collect your IP address.
  • Tally: For contact form functionality (where applicable).

3. Legal Basis

Cookie usage is based on the following legal grounds, in accordance with the GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC) as implemented in Greek law:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Legitimate interest — required for website operation (no consent needed).
  • Analytics and third-party cookies: User consent.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

6. More Information

For more information about how we handle your data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
